What's this all about?

This is all about birthdays! Think about someone you like and how cool it would be if they came to this site on their birthday and there's a birthday card from Arnold and Dusty made out to them at the top of this page. That would be awesome! ...Right? Yes. That would be awesome.

Here's how it works:

It's a very simple. Just follow these steps:

  1. Complete the form below
  2. Click the Submit button

That's it!

You can send a card to your parent, favorite sibling, child, cousin, grandparent, brother-in-law, step-parent, family friend, uncle, co-worker, teammate, boss, bartender, teacher, downstairs neighbor, barista, landlord, mailperson, dog, buddy, homeboy, compadre, bro, mate, pal, companion, amigo, brother from another mother, wingman, significant other, girlfriend, boyfriend, boo, better half, domestic partner, life partner, soul mate, lover, fiance, spouse, ex-spouse .... you get the idea. Basically, you can send a card to just about anyone, as long as you know their birthday.

I guess you could submit your own name and birthday, but think about how good you'll feel by submitting a card for someone else.

There are just two rules to follow...


  1. Only one birthday card per day.

  2. The date you enter should be the date of the persons next birthday, starting one week from today.
